Promotion Process

Includes manager promotion, trainer promotion etc.

Promotion to Manager

  • Foward phone to customer service call group

  • Change CRM to managers

  • Check the Team Switching Box

  • Add to Huds

  • Add MGT to manager name in CMV

See all

Promotion to Trainer

  • Forwarded to Customer Service

  • Forward email to sales manager

  • Update SF to match another trainer

Promotion from Sales to Customer Service (Old Account)

  • Change VS Password Immediatly put PW in Asana

  • Change email password Use same PW as VS Put PW in Asana

  • Downgrade their account Remove Auto Dialing and Call Recording

  • Remove from reps@ or reps2@ email and add to salesmanager@

  • ADFS SAML configuration (For Education)

Promotion from Sales to Customer Service (New Account)

  • Create Alias with first initial.last name and use that for ONLY VS

  • Set Email Password

  • Forward email to Customer Service
